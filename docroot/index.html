About me

PROFILE

My name is Raphaël Larrinaga. I studied webdesign and multimedia at HEAJ in Namur (Belgium). I'm passionate by graphic design and street culture. I like clean and user friendly websites. Simplicity is time saving and won't go out of fashion.

I DO

I DO IT WITH
